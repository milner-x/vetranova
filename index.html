About VetraNova

Founded in 2015, VetraNova began with a simple mission: deliver trustworthy veterinary products to the people who need them most — farmers and local veterinary professionals. We partner with certified manufacturers to bring everything from vaccines and antibiotics to diagnostic tools and nutritional supplements.

Our team travels to farms, listens to challenges, and continuously adapts our catalog. We believe healthy livestock means thriving communities. That's why we invest in education, offering free workshops on animal husbandry and product use.
